Facility Management - Cleaning

Our customers over the years have varied from diverse sectors like corporate offices, schools, fitness centers, hospital as well as luxurious apartments and rural houses and Yashraj Group is proud to have served them dedicatedly. Houses in India can be a victim to a lot of dust and dirt and hence regular house cleaning can go a long way in improving the quality of life while keeping away all disease-causing bacteria and germs

Cleaning can be a tiresome chore, especially for working professionals for whom time is an important asset. Our customers can opt for any type of home cleaning service depending on the need and the budget while anticipating a spic and span house. Yashraj Group best seller, the deep cleaning service will ensure every corner of the house is spotless and pristine while you relax and not let the burden of a dirty house worry you. Yashraj Group also provides specific kitchen or bathroom cleaning, two rooms which require regular attention as compared to other areas of the house. Peculiar cleaning of curtains, mattress or even fridge are also offered as a part of home cleaning services

Furthermore, Yashraj Group caters to specific requests as well like pre-event cleaning service to give your house the festive ambience or after party cleaning service to clear up the mess. In addition, Yashraj Group also provides a unique car cleaning service right at your doorstep with a proper interior and exterior sanitization of the vehicle.
