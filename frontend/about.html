Welcome to Yashraj Business Group

Yashraj Business Group (YBG), founded in 2017, is a dynamic multi-sector enterprise built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and innovation. Established under the visionary leadership of Mr. Vaibhav Amrut Patil (Chairman & Managing Director), the group has rapidly grown into a trusted name across multiple industries including security services, manpower supply, facility management, infrastructure projects, taxation consultancy, and multi-services.

Mr. Patil holds an MBA in Finance & Marketing, an M.Com, and is currently pursuing LLB. Backed by more than a decade of industry experience, he continues to drive the group’s strategic growth and operational excellence.

With its corporate office in Charholi, PCMC, Pune, and registered office in Sangli, Maharashtra, YBG today serves 150+ clients with the support of 500+ employees. The group is steadily expanding its presence across major cities including Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Kolhapur, Sangli, Satara, Jalna, and Amravati. Our vision: PAN-India presence by 2030.

Yashraj Business Group is ISO 9001:2015 certified and holds PSARA licenses across Maharashtra, reinforcing its commitment to quality, compliance, and customer satisfaction.

🚀 Mission Statement

Our mission is to create value through excellence, integrity, and innovation by focusing on the following:

  • Deliver legally approved and quality-certified security, facility, and utility services.
  • Provide end-to-end manpower solutions for industries and infrastructure projects.
  • Offer seamless taxation, licensing, and business consulting.
  • Drive employment generation, rural skill development, and entrepreneurship.
  • Build long-term partnerships rooted in trust, transparency, and innovation.

  • Innovation Adoption of technology and creative problem-solving.

🎯 Vision Statement

“Our vision is to become India’s most trusted multi-sector business group, recognized for excellence, compliance, and customer satisfaction. By leveraging innovation, transparency, and long-term partnerships, we strive to expand our footprint across India and global markets, while contributing to nation-building, employment generation, and social responsibility.”
