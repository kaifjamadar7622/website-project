Welcome to Yashraj Business Group

YASHRAJ BUSINESS GROUP PVT. LTD. (Formerly Known as YASHRAJ SECURITY) is a start-up company founded by Mr.Vaibhav Amrut Patil. Y.B.G. Pvt. Ltd. is one of the most valuable names as a Security guards services, Facility Management, All Taxation works, Man Power Supply & Contract Staffing, Payroll Management, Project Planning & Execution & Multi Service provider in all over Maharashtra with its head office located in Pune, amid an experienced staff that has performed hundreds of assessments with their proven ability. The company was founded in the year 2017. A company incorporates in the year 2020 on 5 September.

YASHRAJ BUSINESS GROUP PVT. LTD. renders cost-effective and trustworthy services which are required for ever changing need in our locations. WE STAND FOR : Credibility, Honesty, Integrity, Equality, Humility, Resiliency. We provide best Security guards services, Facility Management, All Taxation works, Man Power Supply & Contract Staffing, Payroll Management, Project Planning & Execution.

Yashraj Business Group is managed by YBG PVT. LTD, a dynamic and forward-thinking company led by Mr. Vaibhav A. Patil, who serves as the Chairman and Managing Director. With a strong educational background in commerce, finance, marketing, and law, Mr. Patil brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the organization.

The business seeks to acquire capital from an angel investor or Directors and will expand to additional cities.

Business Areas:

YBG Pvt Ltd operates in diverse business sectors, including:

Security Services: Providing professional security solutions to protect assets, properties, and individuals.

Facility Management: Offering comprehensive facility management services to optimize operational efficiency and enhance asset value.

Contract Staffing: Supplying skilled manpower and staffing solutions to meet clients' temporary or project-based staffing needs.

Sanitization Services: Delivering thorough sanitization and disinfection solutions to create safe and hygienic environments.

Taxation Services: Providing expert tax advisory, planning, and compliance services to individuals and businesses.

Multi Services: Offering integrated services tailored to meet clients' diverse requirements across various sectors.
