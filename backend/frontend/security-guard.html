Security Guard

YASHRAJ SECURITY SERVICE AND PLACEMENT SERVICE will be a licensed, insured, and bonded business and will offer unarmed guards, based on client needs. All guards will be certified for security work and to carry firearms. Whenever possible, the same guards will be sent consistently to the same clients. However, substitutes will be necessary from time to time and the business will ensure that detailed data about the job is transmitted to substitute guards on those occasions.

YASHRAJ SECURITY SERVICE AND PLACEMENT SERVICE will build trust with clients as a partner, rather than simply in the specific guard or guards they grow comfortable with. Guards will keep in constant communication with their team via portable two-way radios and with the YASHRAJ SECURITY SERVICE AND PLACEMENT SERVICE office, as necessary through mobile phones.

Physical Guarding (deployment of unarmed and uniformed security guards) is our most frequently offered security service across client types and locations. Our security guards are carefully recruited after thorough background and police verification and are rigorously trained in their duties before being deployed at a customer site.

We are specialist in all below categories and having well trained staff to secure you and your loved ones. Security Services – Security guards, Corporate, industrial, residential, Bank & ATM, IT, Hospital, Mall, Bouncer
