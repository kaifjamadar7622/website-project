Work with Yashraj Business Group

Yashraj Business Group Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian owned and operated private company that provides security guard services, facility management, manpower supply, and taxation services to clients nationwide across all industries. Our team is carefully recruited and well trained—we pride ourselves in being professional, responsive, and reliable.

We are always looking for responsible, hardworking people to join the team here at Yashraj Business Group Pvt. Ltd. We have a high employee retention rate, which we attribute to the following key factors:

  • We value and respect our employees
  • We look to employ professionals from the local communities in which we serve, understanding that they bring specialized knowledge, greater potential for continuity, and a stronger sense of personal investment to the challenge
  • We offer opportunities for advancement
  • Our professionals undergo a rigorous training regimen that includes classroom work
